Why we built it

The hardest problems in F&B aren't on the menu. They live in the opening, equipping, sourcing and staffing of the kitchen behind it.

The decisions that decide whether a restaurant survives, where to open, how to design the kitchen, what to buy and from whom, how to stay licensed and reviewed, sit upstream of every order ticket.

Horeca Store has spent years inside that upstream world: the equipment, the suppliers, the build-outs. The AI Suite is what happens when we point an army of agents at the decisions an operator actually loses sleep over.
